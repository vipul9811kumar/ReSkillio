Common questions
Do I need a credit card to start?
No. The free tier — resume upload, skill extraction, trait archetype, and industry fit scores — requires no card and no account. You only need to pay when you want to download documents or access the detailed gap breakdown.
What exactly is included in the Career Pack?
For one target role: a full skill gap breakdown showing exactly which skills to close, a Gemini-generated career narrative grounded in your actual data, a skills-first resume rewrite (PDF), a cover letter tailored to the role, 5 interview talking points in the employer's language, and a 90-day reskilling roadmap with real course links. You can re-run the analysis for the same target role as many times as you need.
What's the difference between Career Pack and Pro?
Career Pack is a one-time purchase for one specific target role. Pro is a monthly subscription that gives you unlimited role targeting, the Opportunity Radar (which matches you to fractional and consulting opportunities based on your profile), a weekly companion digest that tracks your progress week-over-week, and LinkedIn summary variants. Pro is for people who are in an active 3-month transition and want ongoing intelligence, not just a one-time report.
How does enterprise pricing work for HR teams?
Enterprise is priced per employee processed, typically $50–$200 per head depending on volume and features. This compares to $3,000–$8,000 per employee for traditional outplacement firms. You get white-labelled reports with your branding, a bulk onboarding flow (CSV upload), and an HR analytics dashboard showing cohort-level progress. Reach out to discuss your specific situation — we can usually set up a pilot within a week.
Is my resume data private?
Yes. Your resume is processed to extract skills and stored in your candidate profile. It is never shared with employers, never used to train models, and never sold. In the Opportunity Radar, employers see only an anonymised profile — your actual identity and employer history are revealed only after mutual acceptance of an introduction request.
Will there be local pricing for India, UK, and EU?
Yes — region-specific pricing is coming. Pricing in INR, GBP, and EUR will reflect local purchasing power, not just currency conversion. If you're outside the US and want to be notified when local pricing launches, email vk@vkdesignlabs.com with your region.
Can I use ReSkillio as a demo without paying?
Yes. Add ?demo=true to any URL to activate demo mode, which bypasses the paywall and uses pre-populated data. This is designed for investor demos, HR evaluations, and conference presentations. The demo token is available on request for enterprise evaluations.
